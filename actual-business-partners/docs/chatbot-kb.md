# Chatbot Knowledge Base: Business Partners

## Purpose

Business Partners stores trading companies and contacts used across LumberLinq.

## Navigation

View partners from Business Partners > View Business Partners.

Create a partner from Business Partners > Add Business Partner or by clicking New Partner.

## List Page

The list supports search, status filtering, sorting, row actions, and multi-select deletion.

Visible columns include Name, Party Type, Contact Person, Phone, Tax ID, Registration Type, Country, Status, and Actions.

## Creating Partners

The create form has Identity, Contact & Location, Operations, Attachments, and Bank Accounts tabs.

Attachments require the partner to be saved first.

## Required Fields

Company Name, Party Type, Registration Type, Tax ID or GSTIN, Party Category, Email, Phone 1, Address Line 1, Country, and Zip Code are required.

## Status

Active partners are visible in new transactions.

Inactive partners are hidden from new transactions but retained for history.

## Bank Accounts

Use Bank Accounts to store bank name, account number, IFSC/SWIFT, and currency.

## Suggested Answers

Q: How do I add a Business Partner?

A: Open Business Partners, click New Partner, complete the required Identity and Contact & Location fields, then click Save Partner.

Q: Why can I not upload attachments while creating a partner?

A: Attachments require an existing partner record. Save the partner first, then open the Attachments tab.

Q: What happens when I deactivate a partner?

A: The partner remains in history but is hidden from new transactions.
