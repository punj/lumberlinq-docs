# Business Partners FAQ

## What is a Business Partner?

A Business Partner is a company or contact your organization works with, such as a buyer, seller, exporter, importer, sawmill, trader, or distributor.

## Where are Business Partners used?

They are used in shipments, tallysheets, inventory records, documents, and reports.

## Which fields are required?

Required fields include Company Name, Party Type, Registration Type, Tax ID or GSTIN, Party Category, Email, Phone 1, Address Line 1, Country, and Zip Code.

## What does Party Type mean?

Party Type defines the role of the partner. A partner can be a Buyer, Seller, or Both.

## What is Party Category?

Party Category identifies whether the partner is Domestic or International.

## Why is GSTIN validation shown?

When the selected country is India, LumberLinq validates the tax field as GSTIN.

## Can I upload documents for a partner?

Yes. Save the partner first, then use the Attachments tab to upload documents such as tax certificates, bank confirmation files, and compliance documents.

## What file types are supported for attachments?

Images, PDF, Word, Excel, CSV, PowerPoint, text, ZIP, JSON, and XML files are supported. Each file can be up to 10 MB.

## What happens when I deactivate a partner?

Inactive partners are hidden from new transactions. Existing historical records remain available.

## Can I delete any partner?

No. A partner may not be deletable if it is referenced by active shipments or other records. The owner company cannot be deleted.

## Why do I see a duplicate warning?

LumberLinq checks for existing partners with the same company name or tax ID. If a possible duplicate is found, you can cancel or choose to save anyway.

## Can a partner have multiple bank accounts?

Yes. Use the Bank Accounts tab and click Add Bank Account.
