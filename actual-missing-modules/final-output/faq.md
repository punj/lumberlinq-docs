# Missing Modules FAQ

## Inventory

### What is Inventory used for?
Inventory tracks timber stock after tally sheets are created and before material is shipped or processed.

### Why can I not add a transport unit to a shipment?
The transport unit may not be received into inventory, may already be assigned, or may be in another status such as shipped or in process.

### When should I use an inventory adjustment?
Use adjustments only to correct stock records. Add notes so the reason is clear later.

## Dashboard V5

### What should I check first on Dashboard V5?
Check shipment activity, inventory status, reconciliation status, payment flow, and processing activity.

### Why does Dashboard V5 matter?
It gives managers a quick view of business health without opening every module.

## Transport Units

### What is a Transport Unit?
A Transport Unit is a physical timber lot or unit created from tally sheet measurements.

### Where are Transport Units used?
They are used in tally sheets, inventory, shipments, and reports.

## Utilities

### What does Unit Conversion do?
It converts measurement values between supported units and can keep a list of conversion rows.

### What does Volume Estimate calculate?
It calculates CBM, CFT, quantity totals, and optional price totals.

### What is the Slab Generator for?
It creates timber price slabs from a base rate and slab settings.

## Subscriptions and Plan Limits

### Where can I see my current subscription?
Open `/subscriptions` and view the Current Subscription tab.

### Where can I see payment status?
Open Transaction History in Subscriptions. Payment status appears in each row.

### What do plan limits affect?
Plan limits can affect storage, users, AI credits, tally sheets, shipments, transport units, products, business partners, support level, and analytics.

## Storage

### What is counted in Storage?
Documents and photos uploaded through supported modules can count toward storage usage.

### What does the storage page show?
It shows used space, quota, free space, file count, and the file list.

## Support Tickets

### When should I create a support ticket?
Create a ticket when you need help with a workflow, data issue, access issue, payment issue, or system behavior.

### What should I include in a ticket?
Include the module name, record reference, expected result, actual result, and screenshots where useful.
