# Chatbot Knowledge Base - Missing Modules

## Inventory

Intent: inventory overview, stock status, inventory movement, processing runs, adjustments.

Answer: Inventory tracks timber stock across available, in-process, in-shipment, shipped, and processed states. Use Inventory Overview for stock visibility, In/Out for movement history, and Processing for timber conversion workflows.

## Dashboard V5

Intent: dashboard, business overview, KPI, landing screen.

Answer: Dashboard V5 is the main business overview. It summarizes shipments, inventory, payment flow, reconciliation, loading site utilization, processing runs, financial health, and subscription status.

## Transport Units

Intent: transport unit, add transport unit to shipment, tally transport, inventory receipt.

Answer: A Transport Unit is created from a tally sheet and represents physical timber stock. Receive it into inventory before assigning it to a shipment. If it cannot be linked, check inventory status and whether it is already assigned or shipped.

## Utilities

Intent: unit conversion, volume estimate, slab generator.

Answer: Utilities provide calculation tools. Unit Conversion converts measurements. Volume Estimates calculates CBM, CFT, quantity, and price totals. Slab Generator creates size-based price slabs.

## Subscriptions and Plan Limits

Intent: subscription, plan, payment, invoice, transaction history, package limits.

Answer: Open Subscriptions to view the current plan, transaction history, available packages, checkout, and payment statuses. Plan limits can control usage for storage, users, AI credits, tally sheets, shipments, transport units, products, and business partners.

## Storage

Intent: storage quota, storage usage, files, documents.

Answer: Storage shows used space, quota, free space, file count, and files uploaded across modules such as documents, photos, business partner records, shipment documents, and loading site documents.

## Support Tickets

Intent: support ticket, create ticket, ticket status, help request.

Answer: Support Tickets let users create and track support requests. Use the ticket list for status, ticket detail for replies, and new ticket form to submit a request with category and issue details.
