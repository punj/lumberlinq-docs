# Before and After Script

Before LumberLinq:
Teams check spreadsheets for stock.
Transport units are hard to trace.
Volume and price calculations are repeated manually.
Subscription and storage usage are unclear.
Support issues are tracked outside the system.

After LumberLinq:
Dashboard V5 gives a quick business overview.
Inventory tracks stock movement and processing.
Transport units connect tally sheets to shipments.
Utilities handle unit conversion, volume estimates, and slab pricing.
Subscriptions show plan, payment, and limit information.
Storage shows quota and files.
Support Tickets keep help requests organized.

Result:
Less manual work.
Better visibility.
Cleaner operations for timber and logistics teams.
