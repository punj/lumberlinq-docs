# Before/After Pain-and-Solution Script

## Title

From Spreadsheet Master Data to Centralized Timber Operations

## Before

Your timber team is working from scattered spreadsheets.

Products are named differently by different users. HS codes are hard to verify. Some users select the wrong formula for round logs or sawn timber. Loading yards, mills, ports, and warehouses are stored in separate files or remembered by individual team members.

When the team needs to prepare a tally sheet, container, shipment, or report, they spend time checking old records, asking for site details, and correcting duplicate entries.

The result is slower work, more manual checking, and poor visibility.

## After

With LumberLinq, Products and Loading Sites are maintained as centralized records.

The Products module keeps product name, formula, HS code, species, product code, and description in a searchable catalog. Teams can create, edit, search, validate, and confirm actions from the same page.

The Loading Sites module keeps yards, forests, mills, ports, warehouses, contacts, capacity, machines, operating details, and notes in one searchable place.

Validation helps required information get completed before save. Confirmation dialogs help prevent accidental deletion. Clean records reduce duplicates and support faster daily operations.

## Closing Message

LumberLinq gives timber exporters, importers, traders, logistics teams, and yard supervisors a practical way to move from scattered records to organized operations.
