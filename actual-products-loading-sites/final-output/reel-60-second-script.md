# 60-Second Promotional Reel Script

## Format

Use a calm professional voiceover with actual captured Products and Loading Sites UI.

## Script

0:00-0:06  
On screen: Products list  
Voiceover: In timber operations, small master data problems can become daily delays.

0:06-0:13  
On screen: Product rows with demo products  
Voiceover: Product names, formulas, HS codes, species, product codes, and descriptions need to be accurate and easy to find.

0:13-0:20  
On screen: Product search  
Voiceover: LumberLinq gives your team a centralized Products module with fast search and clear table actions.

0:20-0:29  
On screen: Create Product and validation screenshots  
Voiceover: Add products with the right formula, use ROUND for logs and poles, use SQUARE for sawn timber, and rely on validation for required fields.

0:29-0:36  
On screen: Loading Sites list  
Voiceover: Loading Sites keeps your yards, mills, forests, ports, and warehouses organized in one place.

0:36-0:46  
On screen: Loading Site create form  
Voiceover: Store site name, country, address, contact, operating hours, capacity, machines installed, site type, and notes.

0:46-0:54  
On screen: Edit and delete confirmation screens  
Voiceover: Update records when operations change, and use confirmation dialogs to prevent accidental deletion.

0:54-1:00  
On screen: Products and Loading Sites list views  
Voiceover: LumberLinq helps timber teams reduce spreadsheets, avoid duplicates, and improve visibility across daily logistics.

## Text Overlays

- Centralized product catalog
- Search by product and site details
- Required validation
- Manage loading yards and ports
- Reduce duplicate records
- Improve operational visibility
