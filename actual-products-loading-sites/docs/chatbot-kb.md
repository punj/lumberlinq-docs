# Chatbot Knowledge Base: Products and Loading Sites

## Products

Intent: explain_products
Answer: Products are timber catalog records used across LumberLinq. They store product name, formula, HS code, species, product code, description, and optional photos.

Intent: create_product
Answer: Open Products, choose the add product action, enter a name, select a formula, enter a numeric HS code, add optional species, product code, and description, then save.

Intent: product_formula_help
Answer: Choose Round Log for round logs measured with Hoppus style calculations. Choose Square / Sawn for rectangular or sawn timber measured by length, width, and height.

Intent: search_products
Answer: Open Products and type a keyword in the search box. The list filters to matching product records.

Intent: edit_product
Answer: Open Products, search for the record, open the product row, update the fields, and save.

Intent: delete_product
Answer: Use the delete action from the list or edit page. Confirm the dialog. If the product is linked to operational records, LumberLinq may block deletion.

## Loading Sites

Intent: explain_loading_sites
Answer: Loading Sites are physical locations where timber is stored, processed, staged, or loaded, such as yards, forests, mills, ports, and warehouses.

Intent: create_loading_site
Answer: Open Loading Sites, choose the add action, enter the site name, select country, add optional state, city, address, contact, operating, capacity, machine, site type, and notes, then save.

Intent: search_loading_sites
Answer: Open Loading Sites and type a keyword in the search box. The list filters to matching loading site records.

Intent: edit_loading_site
Answer: Open Loading Sites, search for the site, open the row, update the location or operating details, and save.

Intent: delete_loading_site
Answer: Use the delete action from the list or edit page and confirm the dialog. If the site is linked to containers, measurements, or bills of lading, LumberLinq may block deletion or require extra handling.

Intent: loading_site_required_fields
Answer: Loading Site requires site name and country. Other fields are optional but useful for operations and reporting.
