# 60-Second Promotional Reel Script

Timber logistics teams handle shipments, tally sheets, inventory, payments, partners, yards, and documents every day.

When reporting is spread across spreadsheets, it becomes difficult to know what is shipped, what is pending, who has access, and where money is outstanding.

LumberLinq Reports give practical views for business partners, products, loading sites, transport units, tally sheets, reconciliation, shipments, finance, inventory, users, and app usage.

Use filters, review charts and tables, and export reports to Excel or PDF.

Manage User helps administrators invite team members, review active and inactive users, and manage pending invitations.

With RBAC, each user can get the right access: field operator, supervisor, analyst, manager, or custom permissions.

LumberLinq gives timber and logistics teams clearer reporting and stronger access control.
