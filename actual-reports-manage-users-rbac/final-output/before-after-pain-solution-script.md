# Before / After Pain-and-Solution Script

## Before

Reports are prepared manually from spreadsheets.
Shipment, inventory, payment, and user activity data are checked in different places.
Managers wait for updates.
Field users may have more access than they need.
Finance teams struggle to see overdue and outstanding amounts clearly.

## After

LumberLinq provides dedicated reports for shipments, finance, inventory, reconciliation, tally sheets, users, and app usage.
Teams can filter reports, review charts and tables, and export to Excel or PDF.
Admins can invite users, track pending invitations, and assign role-based access.
RBAC templates and permission groups help each person access only what they need.

## Result

Cleaner reporting, faster review, stronger access control, and less manual follow-up for timber logistics teams.
