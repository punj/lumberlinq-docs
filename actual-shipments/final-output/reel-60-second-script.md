# 60-Second Reel Script

A timber shipment is not just a dispatch entry.

It includes shipper and consignee details, notify party, BL number, buyer order, exporter reference, ports, transport units, inventory movement, shipment documents, invoice value, payments, export reports, and reconciliation.

When these are managed separately, teams waste time and make avoidable mistakes.

LumberLinq Shipments connects the workflow.

Create the shipment, add parties, link inventory-ready transport units, upload documents, track payment status, record payments, export reports, and review shipment details from a read-only view.

Inventory Overview and In/Out movements help teams understand where material is. Reconciliation and inventory reports support operational review.

For timber exporters, importers, traders, and logistics teams, LumberLinq keeps shipment execution clear, traceable, and connected.
