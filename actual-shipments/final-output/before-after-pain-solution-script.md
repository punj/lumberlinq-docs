# Before/After Pain-and-Solution Script

## Before

Shipment details are in one spreadsheet.

BL and invoice documents are in email.

Transport-unit status is checked separately.

Inventory movement is not clearly connected to shipment assignment.

Payments are tracked outside the shipment record.

Reconciliation happens late, after teams have already spent time chasing updates.

## After

LumberLinq connects the shipment workflow.

Users can manage parties, shipment details, linked transport units, documents, financials, payment history, inventory movement, export reports, reconciliation, and sharing from one system.

Transport units must be received into inventory before they are assigned to a shipment, keeping stock movement accurate.

Shipment view lets teams review linked transport units without editing the record.

## Message

Move from scattered shipment records to a connected timber logistics workflow with LumberLinq.
